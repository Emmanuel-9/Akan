Akan Names

Akan is an ethnic group in Ghana along West African Coast. The Akan have an interesting way of giving names. They give names in accordance to the day of the week one was born and gender. Below are tables of the naming lists.

DAY OF THE WEEK MALE AKAN NAMES
Sunday Kwasi
Monday Kwadwo
Tuesday Kwabena
Wednesday Kwaku
Thursday Yaw
Friday Kofi
Saturday Kwame
DAY OF THE WEEK FEMALE AKAN NAMES
Sunday Akosua
Monday Adwoa
Tuesday Abenaa
Wednesday Akua
Thursday Yaa
Friday Afua
Saturday Ama

Below is an application form which is a simple demonstration of the naming system. The form requires the applicant to enter their gender and date of birth. The collected information would be used to determine the Akan name the applicant would have parse.
