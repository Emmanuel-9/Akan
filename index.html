Akan Names

Akan is one of the ethnic groups in Ghana along the West Coast of Africa. As we all know ethnic groups have some cultural ways of doing things. For instance, the Akan have a naming culture. Individuals of the Akan are given names based on the day of the week they were born, but differ in accordance with gender. Here's a list of the names.

DAY OF THE WEEK MALE AKAN NAMES
Sunday Kwasi
Monday Kwadwo
Tuesday Kwabena
Wednesday Kwaku
Thursday Yaw
Friday Kofi
Saturday Kwame
DAY OF THE WEEK FEMALE AKAN NAMES
Sunday Akosua
Monday Adwoa
Tuesday Abenaa
Wednesday Akua
Thursday Yaa
Friday Afua
Saturday Ama
